RTR (A) (Radio Telephony Restricted) License

What is RTR Licence Course

Radio Telephony Restricted (RTR) is a Communication system operated in between Air Crew Member & Pilots to Air Traffic Control and Ground Station. This communication system is very crucial for airline operators. In 1952, India Created Wireless Planning & Coordination (WPC) from the Ministry of Communication

Duration:2 months

Eligibility:12th Science (PCM), Graduation degree in Science/B.Tech (Telecommunication, Electronics, and Electrical)/B.Sc. Aeronautics can join this program. The duration of this program is two months after which candidate has to clear the examination conducted by Wireless Planning & Coordination New Delhi Headquarter, Ministry of Telecommunication - Government of India.

Employment Opportunities:Air Force, Aviation Industry, Domestic Airports, International Airports and Armed Forces, Search and rescue team (investigation of accident and incidences)
