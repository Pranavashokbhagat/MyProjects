B.Sc. Aeronautics (Mechanical / Avionics)

This is a 3 Years full time program affiliated to university of Mumbai. The syllabus of B.Sc. Aeronautics enables the student to acquire the necessary qualification to enter into Job market in any field of Aviation such as Traffic Control, Crew & Manpower scheduling, In-Flight Service Department, Security, Civil Works Department, Line and Major Maintenance Department of Engineering etc. It is also possible that the graduates (B.Sc. Aeronautics) enter the field of Non-Technical Streams in the Aviation Industry like Commercial Department, In-Flight Service Department, Ground Support Department, and Security Department etc. Mechanical Stream Mechanical Stream focuses on the engineering physics, and materials science principles to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems of an aircraft. Subjects in Mechanical stream also include the basic principles of aerodynamics, metallurgy, hardware, propulsion and maintenance practices.

Avionics Stream

Avionics Stream Career Scope Includes: Traffic Control / Antenna Maintenance / Control Displays / Aircraft Lighting System / Communication, Navigation System (CNS), Weather assistance, Flight safety, Broadcasting related to Aviation, Aeronautical Information Services (AIS), etc.
